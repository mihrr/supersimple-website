THE DEPED MISSION

This post is written by Miro B. Ponce

Tamiao Integrated School Region VII Disctrict II Bantayan Cebu is a Public Institution dedicated to maintain excellent academic performance.

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, cultured-based, and complete basic education where:Students learn in a child friendly, gender-sensitive,safe, and motivating environment.Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.Family, community and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.
